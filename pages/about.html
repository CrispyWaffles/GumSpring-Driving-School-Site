Home/About

Friendly. Family-run. Patient by design.

We're a small, Virginia DMV-licensed driving school based in Loudoun County, focused on one thing: turning nervous beginners into safe, confident drivers — one lesson at a time.

Our story

A neighborhood school, built on patience.

We're a small team based right here in Loudoun County, started by local instructors who are passionate about teaching and building confident drivers.

At Gum Spring Driving School our mission is to give every student our undivided attention in a calm car, with someone who wants them to succeed. We operate a fleet of clean, and well-maintained vehicles that are fully equipped with dual controls for your safety.

With over 10+ years of experience in behind the wheel instruction, you are always with an expert who knows how to make you comfortable and at ease.

What we stand for

Our Simple Commitments.

Your pace, not ours.

Some students need 6 lessons. Some need 26. We never push you to the test before you're truly ready.

Straight talk on progress.

After every lesson, you (and your parents, if you're under 18) get an honest update.

All of Loudoun County.

Fully bonded & insured.
